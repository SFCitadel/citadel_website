Visit The Citadel

The SF Citadel is located at:

181 Eddy Street (Between Mason and Taylor Streets)
San Francsico, CA. 94102


Our building is in the heart of downtown, one block from both the Parc 55 Hotel and the Hilton, and three blocks from Union Square.

Parking

There are many nearby parking locations, and some are open 24 hours.
The lot a few steps down the street directly to the east will stay open for events.
Street parking is available as well, however as this is San Francisco, a reminder.... leave nothing in your car. No matter where you park in SF, if you leave anything in your car, someone will want it! A map of parking lots can be found on here on Yelp.

Public Transit

The Powell street BART station is roughly two blocks East on Market street
The Muni F line is roughly two blocks away on Market street There are a large number of buses that stop at the Market and 5th street stations.
The 31 Bus line runs directly down Eddy street.

Accessibility:

Unfortunately, the SFCitadel is not wheelchair accessible as we are located on the second floor with no elevator or lift.
